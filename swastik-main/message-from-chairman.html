Message from Chairman

Honoring Tradition, Embracing the Future

Punit Poudel

Chairman & Founder

Founder & Visionary Leader

25+ Years Industry Pioneer

Design Excellence Award Recipient

Building a Legacy of Excellence

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, our journey continues

Swastik Interiors emerged from a foundational conviction that transcends mere commercial enterprise—a profound understanding that every space possesses inherent potential for transformation into something extraordinary. The inception of our company, over two decades past, was guided by a vision that extends beyond the physical boundaries of interiors to encompass the very essence of human experience within cherished spaces—sanctuaries of home, environments of productivity, and havens of personal reflection.

Our origins trace to a modest atelier, where a devoted cadre of master craftsmen shared an unwavering dedication to the creation of spaces that harmonize beauty with purpose. Beginning with the venerable traditions of furniture creation, we embraced techniques refined across generations, honoring the ancestral wisdom embedded within each methodology. Every creation bore testament not merely to functional necessity, but to the artisan's profound skill and an enduring covenant with the families who would treasure these works for generations hence.

The Janaki Legacy embodies the philosophical cornerstone upon which our enterprise was established. This heritage represents the timeless understanding that authentic beauty emerges from the harmonious synthesis of form and function, tradition and innovation. These principles became the bedrock of our company's identity, establishing values that prioritize narrative richness, memory preservation, and the cultivation of spaces that unite generations in shared experience.

"We don't just create furniture and design spaces; we craft experiences, preserve memories, and build legacies that span generations. Every project is an opportunity to honor the trust our clients place in us and to exceed their highest expectations."

Over the years, we have had the privilege of transforming thousands of spaces across the country. From intimate family homes to grand commercial establishments, each project has taught us something new and reinforced our commitment to excellence. We have witnessed changing trends, evolving technologies, and shifting lifestyle needs, yet our core principles have remained constant: quality, integrity, and unwavering dedication to our clients' satisfaction.

Today, as we stand at the threshold of a new era in interior design, I am more excited than ever about the possibilities that lie ahead. Our team of talented designers, skilled craftsmen, and dedicated professionals continues to push boundaries while honoring our heritage. We embrace new technologies, sustainable practices, and innovative design concepts, all while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that has always defined us.

To our valued clients, past and present, I extend my heartfelt gratitude. Your trust, feedback, and loyalty have been the driving forces behind our growth and success. To those who are considering Swastik Interiors for their next project, I promise you will experience the same dedication, craftsmanship, and care that has defined our work for over two decades.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our founding principles while embracing innovation and growth. Our legacy is not just in the beautiful spaces we create, but in the relationships we build, the traditions we preserve, and the dreams we help realize.

Punit Poudel

Chairman & Founder, Swastik Interiors
